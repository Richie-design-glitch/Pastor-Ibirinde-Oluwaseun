About Me

Pastor Ibirinde Oluwaseun is a dedicated minister of the Gospel serving in the Redeemed Christian Church of God. His passion is to teach the Word of God, inspire believers, and lead people into a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ. Through preaching, counseling, and evangelism, his ministry focuses on spiritual growth, faith, and community transformation.
